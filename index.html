Campus Eats

Hello! Welcome to my food blog. I created this blog to introduce students to underrated and great restaurants that are close to campus. I will tell you about three of my favorite restaurants to go to when I'm tired of going to the EUC or the cafeteria. I hope you find some new places to try out!

Crazy Mexico

Crazy Mexico is a family-run restaurant located in 332 Tate Street, Greensboro. They serve authentic mexican cuisine and have a great variety of dishes to choose from.

Tacos Con Carne
Tacos with steak is one of their most popular dishes.

Crazy Burrito Platter
This burrito platter is covered with meat and smothered with sauce crema (alfredo-like sauce).

Mexican Dessert

This dessert is a fried tortilla with cinnamon and sugar, topped with ice cream and chocolate syrup.

Romeo's Vegan Burgers

Romeo's Vegan Burgers is a vegan restaurant located in 201 S Elm St, Greensboro. They serve delicious burgers using plant-based protein and other vegan dishes.

Vegan Burger

Their vegan burgers are made with a plant-based patty, lettuce, tomato, and vegan mayo and cheese. It's so delicious that you forget it's vegan!

Vegan Chili Fries
These fries are topped with vegan chili and vegan cheese. This is one of their most popular items for a reason!

Vegan peanut butter shake
This vegan peanut butter shake is made with almond milk and vegan ice cream. It's so creamy and delicious it will bring all the boys to the yard!

The Upper Deck Kitchen & Cocktails

The Upper Deck Kitchen & Cocktails is a sports bar located in 948 A Walker Avenue. It's a great place to eat a delicious meal and watch the big game!

The Mac Burger
This burger is topped with mac n cheese, bacon, more cheddar cheese, and a special sauce. It's a must-try!

Fried chicken and Mac
This dish is fried chicken tenders served on top of mac and cheese. The chicken is so crispy and juicy, and the mac and cheese is seasoned to perfection.

Chicken and Waffles
This dish is crispy fried chicken served on top of a fluffy waffle, covered in sliced fruit and heavily sprinkled with powdered sugar. It's a perfect combination of sweet and savory.
